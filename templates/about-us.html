About Us

"To date, 52,834 wildfires have burned 9,539,554 acres, the second most acres burned in the past 10 years." - NIFC.gov

The Active Fire Map was created November 2020 to enable a more user-friendly experience while researching the current locations of potential fires. The site currently is in early-alpha, and any feedback would be greatly appreciated! Our features currently in development are as follows:

  • Check for fires within radius of an address. (Ex: "There are 10 potential fires near you." )
  • Support for loading of only specific Countries, or area.
